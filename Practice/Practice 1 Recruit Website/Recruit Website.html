Introduction

Are you passionate about technology and constantly seeking to improve your skill set? Are you an experienced Python Developer looking to work on exciting projects whilst contributing to the community? Then you are the type of person we are looking for!

Responsibilities

Write reusable, testable, and effective code.

Develop back-end components and improve the functionality of the system.

Test and debug programs.

Integration of user-facing elements.

Implementation of security and data protection.

Integration of data storage solutions.

Coordinate with internal teams to understand user requirements and provide technical solutions.

Requirements

Experience or Knowledge in Python Developement.

Preferably hands on experience in one or two Python frameworks (Django, Flask, or Pyramid).

Familiarity with some ORM (Object-Relational Mapping) libraries.

Knowledge of front-end technologies (JavaScript, HTML5, CSS3).

Exposure to server-side templating languages (Jinja 2, Mako).

Basic knowledge in Odoo / ERP tools would be advantageous.

Some exposure to AWS is nice to have but not essential.

A proactive approach to problem solving.

Tertiary qualifications in IT.

Why volunteer with us

If you relish working in an evolving environment, have a desire to work collaboratively amongst a wealth of talent and gain some great experience personally and professionally our organisation is a great place to be.

We will provide you with a great work environment and support by providing you with advanced systems to work on and great mentorship, which will give you an opportunity to gain experience and improve your skill sets.

You will get the opportunity to meet volunteers with similar interests, which will provide you with an opportunity to network and learn from other professionals.

Most importantly, if you volunteer with us for four months and beyond, we will provide you with Volunteering Certificate and references on your LinkedIn Profile
